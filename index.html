6-week timeline — web MVP

Paced to be genuinely achievable in six weeks with roughly four developers plus dedicated QA. The two riskiest pieces are de-risked deliberately: Stripe billing starts early and runs as a parallel track, and QA runs continuously rather than only at the end. Scope is the web app across all three portals; native mobile and Phase 2 items are a fast-follow, not in-window.
